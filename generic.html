Ash Neo

I believe that anything can be achieved if you put your heart and soul into it.

Hello! I'm Ash Neo, an aspiring marketing and business analyst who enjoys turning complex data into clear, actionable insights that help businesses make smarter decisions. With 6 years of professional experience working as a Paraplanner and completing my BSc Marketing (Analytics focus) at the Singapore University of Social Sciences (graduating November 2025), I've built hands-on experience in SQL, Python, and Tableau through high-distinction academic projects and real world portfolio work.

I love exploring what drives customer behaviour and business performance, then translating these insights into strategies that create real impact. Outside of work, I enjoy staying active at the gym, exploring new tech skills, trying out new food recipes, and learning Japanese.
